Hello!

I am a postdoctoral associate at MIT linguistics. I received my PhD in the same department in 2024. My dissertation investigated "unconventional" case phenomena like case-marked adverbials. Before coming to MIT, I studied for a B.A. and M.A. in linguistics at the Department of Linguistics at Seoul National University. You can reach me at jou @ mit.edu .
