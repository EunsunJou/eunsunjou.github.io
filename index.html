Hello!

In 2024, I received my Ph.D. in linguistics at Department of Linguistics and Philosophy at MIT. My dissertation investigated "unconventional" case phenomena such as case-marked adverbials and case stacking. Before my Ph.D, I studied for a B.A. and M.A. in linguistics at the Department of Linguistics at Seoul National University. You can reach me at eunsun.jou @ gmail.com .
