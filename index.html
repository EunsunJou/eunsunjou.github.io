I am a third year Ph.D. student at the department of linguistics at MIT. My research interests mainly lie in syntax and its interface with morphology and pragmatics. I am also interested in computational phonology and learnability -- in my most recent project, I suggest an alternative algorithm that learns hidden metrical structure as an amendment to Robust Interpretive Parsing. Before coming to MIT, I studied for a B.A. and M.A. in linguistics at the department of linguistics at Seoul National University.

You can reach me at jou at mit dot edu.

Ongoing Projects

  • Analysis of honorification as agree, with a focus on Korean but also cross-linguistically
  • An economy-based amendment to Robust Interpretive Parsing, based on the Gradual Learning Algorithm
    My implementation of the GLA and the amendment is available on Github.
